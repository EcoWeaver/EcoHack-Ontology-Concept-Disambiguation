A new species of Zagrammosoma (Hymenoptera: Eulophidae) from the Indo-Australian Region, a parasitoid of the invasive pest species Liriomyza huidobrensis (Diptera: Agromyzidae)
Zagrammosoma latilineatum Ubaidillah, sp. nov. is described from Indonesia and Australia (Queensland). It has been reared from Liriomyza huidobrensis, an introduced pest of potatoes and other crops in Southeast Asia. This is the first record of Zagrammosoma from Southeast Asia. The biology of Zagrammosoma species is reviewed.
