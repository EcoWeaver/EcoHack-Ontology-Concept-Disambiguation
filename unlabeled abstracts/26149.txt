First record of Sphoeroides spengleri (Osteichthyes: Tetraodontidae) in the Mediterranean Sea
The capture of a specimen of Sphoeroides spengleri (Osteichthyes: Tetraodontidae), 17 December 2000 and 29*7 mm total length, from the Malaga coast (Alboran Sea, western Mediterranean) represents the first record of a new alien species for Mediterranean waters.
