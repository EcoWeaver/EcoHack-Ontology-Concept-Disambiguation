New or little-known species of exotic Tipulidae (Diptera). XV
Descriptions are given of three new species of Pediciine crane flies, two belonging to the genus Ula and one to Dicronoto, all from Assam, and of five further species of Limoniini, all in the genus Antocha. The materials were collected in the Himalayan region from Kumaon in the west to Sikkim and Assam.
