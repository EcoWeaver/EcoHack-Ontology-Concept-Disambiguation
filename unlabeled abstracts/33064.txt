Aggressive behavior of Apis mellifera L. workers towards introduced queens. 3. Relationship between the attractiveness of the queen and worker aggression
The attractiveness of virgin queens of five age groups was determined using a new bioassay technique; queen attractiveness increased with age. By introducing queens of these age groups to small cages of worker bees, a direct relationship was found between queen attractiveness and worker aggression. The significance of these results is discussed.
