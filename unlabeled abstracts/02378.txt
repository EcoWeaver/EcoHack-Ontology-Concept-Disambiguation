A Review of Cadra Walker in Australia: Five New Native Species and the Two Introduced Pest Species (Lepidoptera: Pyralidae: Phycitinae)
Five new species of Cadra Walker, C. reniformis, C. perfasciata, C. corniculata, C. acuta and C. rugosa, are described from Australia, and the two cosmopolitan pest species present, C. cautella (Walker) and C. figulilella (Gregson), are figured. the native Australian species form a monophyletic group within the genus. Their biology is unknown. They have never been recorded from stored products, though the distribution of several species coincides with well-populated regions.
