Invasive plant species of the areas of the Belogorye nature reserve
V rezul'tate mnogoletnikh issledovanii s 1992 po 2021 gg. vpervye byl sostavlen spisok invazionnykh rastenii dlia vsekh piati uchastkov zapovednika <<Belogor'e>> (Belgorodskaia oblast'), vkliuchaiushchii 70 vidov sosudistykh rastenii. Naibol'shee kolichestvo invazionnykh vidov predstavleno na uchastke <<Les na Vorskle>> (62, 14 iz kotorykh vkhodiat v TOP-100 invazionnykh vidov Rossii), a naimen'shee - na uchastke <<Ostras'evy Iary>> (23, 9 iz kotorykh vkhodiat v TOP-100). Rospotrebnadzor dlia territorii RF ukazyvaet 6 vidov i 1 rod (Cuscuta spp.). Tol'ko dva vida otmechaiutsia na uchastkakh zapovednika <<Belogor'e>>: Cuscuta campestris Yuncker - vstrechaiushchiisia na chetyrekh iz piati uchastkakh (krome uchastka <<Lysye Gory>>) i Ambrosia artemisiifolia L. - vstrechaiushchiisia tol'ko na uchastkakh <<Les na Vorskle>> i <<Stenki-Izgor'ia>>. As a result of many years of research from 1992 to 2021, a list of invasive plants was compiled for the first time for all five sites of the Belogorye Reserve (Belgorod Region), including 70 species of vascular plants. The largest number of invasive species is represented on the site <<Les na Vorskle>> (62, 14 of which are in the TOP 100 invasive species of Russia), and the smallest - on the site <<Ostrasevy Yary>> (23, 9 of which are in the TOP 100). Rospotrebnadzor for the territory of the Russian Federation indicates 6 species and 1 genus (Cuscuta spp.). Only two species are recorded in the areas of the Belogorye Reserve: Cuscuta campestris Yuncker - found in four of the five sites (except for the <<Lysye Gory>> site) and Ambrosia artemisiifolia L. - found only in the areas of <<Les na Vorskle>> and <<Stenki-Izgorya>>.
