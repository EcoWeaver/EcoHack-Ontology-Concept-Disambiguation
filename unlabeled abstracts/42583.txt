Appendiseta robiniae (Gillette, 1907) (Hemiptera: Drepanosiphidae) -- a new invasive species of aphids in Donbass
Appendiseta robiniae (Gillette, 1907) (Aphidoidea: Drepanosiphidae) is newly recorded from Donbass Region as invasive species of aphids collected in October 2018 on Black locust (Robinia pseudoacacia L.) in green spaces of Donetsk. The data on the modern distribution, biology and harmfulness of A. robiniae in the conditions of the secondary area are given.
