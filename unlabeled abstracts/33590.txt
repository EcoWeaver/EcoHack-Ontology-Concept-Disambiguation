NEW OR LITTLE-KNOWN SPECIES OF EXOTIC TIPULIDAE (DIPTERA) IV
SynopsisA new species of Malaisemyia is described from material from Manipur. One species of Dicranota (Rhaphidolabina) from Assam and two species of the same subgenus from Sikkim are described as new. Four new species of Dicranota (Rhaphidolabis) are described from material from Sikkim.
