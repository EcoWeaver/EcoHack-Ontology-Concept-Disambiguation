Status of alien plant invasions in the North-eastern region of India.
Abstract This chapter describes the diversity of invasive alien species in the north-eastern region of India (Arunachal Pradesh, Assam, Manipur, Meghalaya, Mizoram, Nagaland, Sikkim and Tripura, and the three northern districts in West Bengal, i.e. Darjeeling, Jalpaiguri and Coochbehar), their dominance patterns, and the mechanisms facilitating the dominance in agricultural systems, forest systems and grasslands/marginal lands.
