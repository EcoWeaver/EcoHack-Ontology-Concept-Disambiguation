Hexasepalum teres (Rubiaceae): A potential New World invasive alien weed from Eastern Ghats
Hexasepalum teres (Walter) J.H.Kirkbr. (Rubiaceae), a Neotropical invasive weed, is reported here for the first time from Eastern Ghats. The occurrence of this species raises alarm of its rapid invasion.
