First record of the black bullhead Ameiurus melas (Teleostei: Ictaluridae) in Poland
The North American catfish, the black bullhead Ameiurus melas, is recorded for the first time in Poland. The origin of these fish is not clear, but their presence may be associated with unregulated introductions by anglers. (c) 2010 The Authors. Journal compilation
