First record of the invasive gecko, Lepidodactylus lugubris Dumeril &amp; Bibron, 1836 in mainland Chile (Squamata, Gekkonidae)
In Chile, the presence of Mourning Gecko,Lepidodactylus lugubrisDumeril &amp; Bibron, 1836 has been recognized for Easter Island (Rapa Nui) from late 19thcentury. Here, we report the first observation of a juvenile specimen ofL. lugubrisin an urban zone of Santiago, Region Metropolitana, mainland Chile, representing the southernmost non-insular record in America for this invasive species. Moreover, an updated distributional map ofL. lugubrisfor South America and the Antilles is provided.
