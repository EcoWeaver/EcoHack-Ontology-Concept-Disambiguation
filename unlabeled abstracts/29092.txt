New records of the invasive crabs Callinectes sapidus Rathbun, 1896 and Percnon gibbesi (H. Milne Edwards, 1853) along the Italian coasts
Callinectes sapidus (Crustacea, Decapoda, Portunidae) and Percnon gibbesi (Crustacea, Decapoda, Percnidae) are respectively reported from two Italian localities: Basento river (Basilicata, Ionian Sea) and Montecristo Island, Tuscan Archipelago (Northern Tyrrhenian Sea). Data on collection sites and discussion regarding their present distribution are provided.
