Isodontia mexicana (de Saussure, 1867) (Hymenoptera: Sphecidae), a new invasive species of digger wasps in the fauna of Donbass
Isodontia mexicana (de Saussure, 1867) is newly recorded from Donbass region (Donetsk Botanical Garden) as invasive species of digger wasps collected in 2015. The history of I. mexicana expansion in Europe is analyzed.
