First record of Butis koilomatodon (Bleeker, 1849) (Gobiiformes: Eleotridae) for the Maranhao state, northeastern Brazil: a case of bioinvasion
The invasive eleotrid species Butis koilomatodon (Bleeker, 1849) is recorded for the first time in the Maranhao state, on Aracagy Beach in Sao Luis, northeastern Brazil.
