New distributional record for two exotic invasive species: Aeschynomene americana L. and Calopogonium mucunoides Desv. in Tripura, North East India
Weeds are the undesirable plants that are grow in the vicinity of native plants. Generally they are highly competitive as well as adaptive in nature. The present paper deals with the new distributional record of two leguminous plants entitled Aeschynomene americana and Calopogonium mucunoides for the Tripura state.
