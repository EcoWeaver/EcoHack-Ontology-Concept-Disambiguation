ATTAGENUS BRUNNEUS FALDERMANN, 1835 (COLEOPTERA: DERMESTIDAE), A NEW INVASIVE SPECIES OF CARPET BEETLES IN THE FAUNA OF BELARUS
For the first time for Belarus, the actual material collected in the territory of the city of Gomel is given for the alien species of carpet beetles Attagenus brunneus Faldermann, 1835 (Coleoptera: Dermestidae) - a dangerous pest of stored food products and materials of plant and animal origin. Information on current distribution, as well as features of biology and ecology of this species is presented.
