Changes in the life history of Abbottina rivularis in Lake Fuxian
After invading Lake Fuxian, China, Abbottina rivularis showed marked changes in its life history traits, including alterations in annuli formation, extension to the length of the breeding season, and an increase in fecundity and growth rate. The results are explained in the context of the enemy release hypothesis.
