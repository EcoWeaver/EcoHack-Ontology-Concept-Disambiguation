Bilan 2008 de l'invasion de Vespa velutina Lepeletier en France (Hymenoptera, Vespidae)
The asiatic hornet Vespa velutina, which was introduced before 2004 already spread throughout almost all the south-west of France. After extensive data validation, nest record registered on the INPN (MNHN) data base enabled to draw the annual spread of the invasive species. We describe here a data validation criteria and assess the evolution of the number and distribution of recorded nests from 2004 to 2008.
