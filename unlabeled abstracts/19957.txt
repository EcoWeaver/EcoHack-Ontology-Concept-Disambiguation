Non-indigenous bivalve -- the Atlantic rangia Rangia cuneata -- in the Wisla Smiala River (coastal waters of the Gulf of Gdansk, the southern Baltic Sea)
Abstract The present paper reports on the occurrence of the Atlantic rangia Rangia cuneata in the Wisla Smiala River (coastal waters of the Gulf of Gdansk, the southern Baltic Sea) from around 2012-2014.
