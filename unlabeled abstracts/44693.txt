THE INVASIVE SPECIES OF PLANTS IN THE FLORA OF THE VICINITIES OF THE SALIKHOVO VILLAGE (CHISHMIN DISTRICT OF THE REPUBLIC OF BASHKORTOSTAN)
Proveden analiz adventivnykh vidov okrestnostei naselennogo punkta Bashkirskogo Predural'ia. Uchastie zanosnykh vidov vo flore 23,5, chto opredeliaet stepen' ee adventizatsii. Vidy proanalizirovany po vremeni i sposobu immigratsii, po stepeni naturalizatsii. Iz florogeneticheskogo spektra ochevidno preobladanie vidov iuzhnogo proiskhozhdeniia (iranoturanskikh, sredizemnomorskikh). Preobladaiut dve zhiznennye formy terofity (65,6) i gemikriptofity (27,1), vysokii uroven' terofitizatsii ukazyvaet na narushennost' izuchennoi territorii. Iz chisla adventov vydeleno 27 agressivnykh invazivnykh vidov, vkhodiashchikh v Chernyi spisok flory Respubliki Bashkortostan. The analysis of adventive species of the surroundings of the settlement of the Bashkir CisUrals is carried out. The participation of alien species in the fl ora is 23.5, which determines the degree of adventization. Species are analyzed by time and method of immigration, by the degree of naturalization. From the phlorogenetic spectrum, the predominance of species of southern origin (IranianTuranian, Mediterranean) is obvious. Two life forms predominate therophytes (65.6) and hemicryptophytes (27.1), a high level of thermophytization indicates the disturbance of the studied territory. Of the adventive groups, 27 invasive invasive species are included in the Black List of the fl ora of the Republic of Bashkortostan.
