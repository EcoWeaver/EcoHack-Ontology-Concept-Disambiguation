EXTENSAO DE OCORRENCIA DO CAMARAO NAO NATIVO Penaeus monodon FABRICIUS, 1798 (DECAPODA: PENAEIDAE) NO LITORAL AMAZONICO BRASILEIRO = THIS STUDY PROVIDES NEW RECORDS OF THE NON-NATIVE SPECIES Penaeus monodon FABRICIUS, 1798, IN THE BRAZILIAN AMAZON COAST THROUGH CATCHES ON STATE OF MARANHAO
O presente estudo fornece tres novos registros de ocorrencia da especie nao nativa Penaeus monodon Fabricius, 1798 no Litoral Amazonico Brasileiro, a partir de capturas no estado do Maranhao.AbstractThis study provides new records of the non-native species Penaeus monodon Fabricius, 1798, in the Brazilian Amazon Coast through catches on State of Maranhao.
