The first record of the invasive lady beetle Harmonia axyridis (Pallas, 1773) (Coleoptera: Coccinellidae) in Turkey
The morphologically variable multicoloured Asian lady beetle, Harmonia axyridis, is reported for the first time from Turkey. A single specimen of this invasive species was recorded in Cappadocia (Central Turkey). General information on the bionomy and distribution of H. axyridis is given.
