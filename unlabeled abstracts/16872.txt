The topmouth gudgeon Pseudorasbora rarva (Temminck et Schlegel, 1846) (Actinopterygii: Cyprinidae): A new species in the ichthyofauna of the Republic of Abkhazia
The data on a finding of the topmouth gudgeon in an irrigation canal of the Kodori River in the Republic of Abkhazia in August of 2015 are presented.
