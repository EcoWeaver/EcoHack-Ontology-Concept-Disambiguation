Additions and notes to the alien flora of the Mordovian State Nature Reserve
From the inventory of flora of the Mordovian State Nature Reserve in 2012, 27 new alien species are identified. In addition, three species are excluded from the flora of the Mordovian Reserve; four species are found in areas adjacent to the reserve and could be registered in its territory in the future; five species that are being cultivated but do not run wild in the territory of the reserve are indicated for the flora of the Mordovian State Nature Reserve for the first time.
