Alien species of the Apiaceae family in the flora of Khabarovsk Territory (the Russian Far East)
The results of studying alien species of the Apiaceae family in the flora of the Khabarovsk Territory are presented. According to our data, 13 alien species of this family, including the invasive species Pastinaca sylvestris are now widespread in the territory of the region. They differ in the degree of naturalization, phytocenotic confinement, and the intensity of settlement.
