OCCURRENCE OF THE NON-NATIVE MUZZLED BLENNY OMOBRANCHUS PUNCTATUS IN A TIDAL POOL OF SANTOS BAY, SOUTHEASTERN BRAZIL
The presence of two specimens of Omobranchus punctatus (Blenniidae), on the center coast of Sao Paulo state, was reported for the first time inside the Bay of Santos. It is another important record related to the distribution of this alien species along the Brazilian coastline.
