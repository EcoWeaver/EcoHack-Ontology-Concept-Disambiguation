The first record of the introduced land snail Bradybaena similaris (Ferussac, 1822) (Mollusca: Heterobranchia: Camaenidae) from Bangladesh
Bradybaena similaris (Ferussac, 1822) is an invasive land snail species native to East and Southeast Asia. Here we report the first record of B. similaris in Bangladesh, an introduced species in this country.
