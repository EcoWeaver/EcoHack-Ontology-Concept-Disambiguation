Erigeron sumatrensis (Asteraceae), casual alien new to the Polish flora / Erigeron sumatrensis (Asteraceae) - nauja atsitiktinai patekusi svetimzeme Lenkijos floros rusis
Abstract Erigeron sumatrensis is newly reported as casual alien in Poland. A single plant was found growing on the edge of the roadside in Glogoczow near Mogilany, Lesser Poland Voivodeship, southern Poland. The locality was mapped by using the ATPOL cartogram method, and the pathway of introduction by road transport was presented.
