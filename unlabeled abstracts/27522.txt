ECOLOGY: Sparrow Wars, Reptilian Eucalypts, and Xenophobes
American Perceptions of Immigrant and Invasive Species . Strangers on the Land. By Peter A. Coates . University of California Press, Berkeley, 2006. 266 pp. $39.95, PS26.95. ISBN 9780520249301. Coates examines the reactions of 19th- and early-20th-century Americans to nonnative species such as starlings, gypsy moths, and eucalyptus.
