Rhinogobiuscf. similisGill, 1859, a goby new to the fish fauna of Iran and the problem of alien invasions
A species of goby, Rhinogobius cf. similis, is reported and described as an alien species from the Hari River basin of Iran. Other potential invaders of this river basin are suggested and their impact discussed.
