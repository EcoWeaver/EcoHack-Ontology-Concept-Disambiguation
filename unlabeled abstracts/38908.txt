First report of predation by a small shark on the invasive rapa whelk Rapana venosa (Valenciennes, 1846) in Argentinean waters
The first case of predation by a small shark Mustelus schmitti on Rapana venosa is reported in Argentinean waters. Rapana venosa is an exotic gastropod that invaded the Rio de la Plata and Atlantic coast of Argentina. This finding could give information about on the trophic control over R. venosa, and their consequences in the dispersion of this mollusk.
