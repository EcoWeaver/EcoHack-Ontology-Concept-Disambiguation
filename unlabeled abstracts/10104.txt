The Native Species of Gossypium (Malvaceae) in Africa, Arabia and Pakistan
12 species of Gossypium are recognised as being native in the area. Of these G. trifurcatum Vollesen is described as new and the following new combinations are made: G. bricchettii (Ulbrich) Vollesen, G. anomalum Wawra & Peyr. subsp. senarense (Wawra & Peyr.) Vollesen, G. herbaceum L. subsp. africanum (Watt) Vollesen.
