A new record of the introduced seaweed Undaria pinnatifida (Laminariales, Phaeophyceae) from the Cantabrian Sea (northern Spain) with comments on its establishment
Undaria pinnatifida was recorded for the third time at the Cantabrian Sea, northern Spain. This record represents the easternmost occurrence of this non-indigenous seaweed along the Spanish coast. Information on the establishment of the species is also provided.
