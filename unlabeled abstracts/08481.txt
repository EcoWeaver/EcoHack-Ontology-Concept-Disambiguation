Invasive species. California tries to rub out the monster of the lagoon.
SAN DIEGO-- In a planet increasingly flooded with invasive species, victories are rare. But a dogged team of scientists and resource managers in California is hoping to beat the odds and triumph over the world's most formidable seaweed. With Caulerpa taxifolia a global threat, the world is closely watching the eradication effort here.
