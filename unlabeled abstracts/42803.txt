Biological invasions as an element of anthropogenic pressure upon the aquatic biotic communities in Lake Khanka
Data on biological invasions of aquatic organisms in Lake Khanka are presented. Sources of invasions in the lake are examined. Characteristics of various species of aquatic animals that settled in the lake are given. Types of influences of invasion processes on the ecosystem of the waterbody are presented.
