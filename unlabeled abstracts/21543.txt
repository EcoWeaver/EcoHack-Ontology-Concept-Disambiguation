New records of Corbicula fluminea (Muller, 1774) in Galicia (Northwest of the Iberian Peninsula): Mero, Sil and Deva rivers
This paper presents new records of Asian clam Corbicula fluminea in the rivers Mero, Sil and Deva. Records from the Rivers Sil and Deva showed the increase of its spread in the Mino river basin. Findings in the Mero basin represented the first Iberian record of this species outside the Mino basin. Furthermore, it was the most north-western record in the Iberian Peninsula.
